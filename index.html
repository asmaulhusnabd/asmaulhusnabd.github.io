About

Currently, I am doing M.Sc.(Thesis-based) in Computing Science at the University of Alberta. I am also a Graduate Teaching Assistant of UofA. I have served as a Lecturer at the department of Information Technology in University of Information Technology & Sciences, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Prior to joining UITS, I was a part-time lecturer for computer programming in Bangladesh University of Textiles. I have accomplished my BSc in Computer Science and Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology. During my undergraduate program, I have attended many online contest and some onsite contest like National Girls Programming Contest(NGPC) and ACM-ICPC Dhaka Regional.

My researh interests are Natural Language processing, Machine Learning, and Data Science.
