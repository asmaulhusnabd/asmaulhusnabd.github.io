I am serving as a Lecturer and Programming Contest Mentor at the department of Information Technology in University of Information Technology & Sciences, Dhaka, Bangladesh.
I am interested in the research of Data Mining, Software Engineering and Intelligent Systems, Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision. I am partially interested in Artificial Neural Network and Data Science. I had attended many online contest and some onsite contest like National Girls Programming Contest(NGPC) and ACM-ICPC Dhaka Regional.
